About the Department

The Department of Mathematics at VVITU, Nambur, Guntur, is dedicated to advancing mathematical frontiers through cutting-edge research, exceptional education, and fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Our Mission

To advance mathematical frontiers through cutting-edge research, to provide an exceptional education that fosters critical thinking and problem-solving, and to cultivate an inclusive community dedicated to mathematical inquiry and literacy.
