DSA

Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) are the fundamental building blocks of computer science. Data Structures, like arrays, trees, and graphs, are specialized formats for organizing and storing data to enable efficient access and modification. Algorithms are well-defined procedures that use these structures to solve problems, such as sorting data or searching for an item. A strong grasp of DSA is crucial for developing optimized, scalable, and high-performance software, forming the core of effective problem-solving and computational thinking for any software engineer.
